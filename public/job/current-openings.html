  • UX Designer

    UX Designer

    Job Type: Full time  Experience: 10+ years Hyderabad

    Job Description:

  • Work with product managers/owners and developers to plan and design the entire user experience for the product – including interface, interaction and flow aspects.
  • Identify user centred design patterns, establish scenarios, place designs into user context and create interactions that align to those requirements.
  • Participate in discussions relative to the future direction of design and user experience for data and analytics products.
  • Research and evaluate emerging design, technology, industry, and market trends
  • Understand user feedback and incorporate the learnings into improving product user experience.
  • Develop wireframes and prototypes around customer needs. Visual design, including composition, layout, information hierarchy, typography, and colour.
  • Extensively prototyping from paper prototyping to interactive prototypes and creating and maintaining a pattern library of web styles, artefacts, and components.

    • Key Skills:

  • Thorough in UX principles, design systems, prototyping, research, Interaction Design, and UI patterns.
  • Deep understanding of UI architecture and user flows.
  • Problem-solving aptitude, strong business insights and customer orientation.
  • Excellent communication skills.
  • Comfortable in working with design tools – Figma, Photoshop, Sketch etc.
  • Understanding of technical implementation of UI patterns (HTML, CSS, JS) is plus.
  • Experience of working with enterprise products is preferable.
